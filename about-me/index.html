Who I am

I’m a visual artist and designer and I find interest in all things! I read, observe and absorb life’s beauty in its entirety. I try to observe hidden patterns in nature and translate them through my paintings, drawings, creations, and process. I believe that outside of conventional education, you have room to explore new methods and ideas through fresh eyes.

 

What I do

I experiment with oil paints, dry and oil pastels, acrylic, collage, inks, watercolor, gouge, clay sculpting , mural painting, photography, performance, web design, graphic design.
